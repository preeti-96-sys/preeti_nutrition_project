Reasons for WEIGHT GAIN and routine

    Unhealthy lifestyle may be due to these following reasons:

  • Consumption of unhealthy diet
  • High stress environment
  • Diet high in trans fat, sugar
  • Excessive alcohol and smoking
  • Physically inactive
  • Insufficient sleep

      • How can you maintain a healthy routine??

  • Be physically active for minimum of 30 minutes a day
  • Consume a well-balanced, low-fat diet with fruits, vegetables and whole grains in majority
  • Eat the quantity of food that your digestive system is capable of digesting
  • Avoid smoking or quit if you do, consult a health care provider for assistance
  • If you consume alcohol, drink in moderation
  • Maintain a proper basic hygiene
  • Oral hygiene is equally important, brush your teeth twice a day
  • Get enough amount of Vitamin D
  • Minimize screen time as much as possible
